Fine Learning

Learn Via Experiments was founded in 2016 by a few high school students as an initiative to expose students to a world of practical knowledge. Understanding that school curriculum mainly centers around theoretical knowledge and facts, Learn Via Experiments was created to let students learn by means of experimenting and practical application.

A key aspect of the initiative is for students to gain mastery of concepts, and use their knowledge and skill to teach other individuals, who also feel explainations based solely on theory are difficult to comprehend.
